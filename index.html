Dr. Martin Felle

Zurich-based Technology Professional

Biography

Moving ultra-high-tech research into the mainstream is not easy. Today, Martin supports organisations seeking to climb out of this ever-deepening technological complexity, by building strong and scalable strategies, tools and content that impress clarity, credibility and quality upon their users. All to turn amazing ideas hardware into impactful products.

Martin is a technology professional bringing extensive experience and expertise in quantum technology research, security advisory, project management and marketing & commercial strategy. Beginning his career as a PhD researcher on applications for cutting-edge quantum communication technologies, going on to transform international organisations across a spectrum of industries in his role as a cybersecurity consultant, his strong and varied background has lead to professional and commercial success in his current role commercializing quantum technologies. These quantum technologies promise amazing advances in security, computing, scientific research, metrology and manufacturing, lifting our world to new and sustainable heights.
