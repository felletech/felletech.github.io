Dr. Martin Felle

Zurich-based Technology Professional

Biography

Martin Felle is a Technology Professional bringing extensive expertise in quantum optics research, III-V semiconductor material growth & characterization, and business risk management. Beginning his career as a PhD researcher on applications for cutting-edge cryptographic technologies, developing InAs/GaAs and InAs/InP single-photon sources, he has gone on to deliver an array of successful and impactful advisory, implementation and testing projects across a spectrum of industries, in particular manufacturing and financial services.
